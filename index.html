Briefly about the current methods of car protection

Problem

Keyless access. The radio signal of the smart key can be transmitted over long distances via GSM operators. The standard system will open the car doors to anyone with a simple tech. equipment.

Decision

Blocking the radio channel. Smart Key works only with a third-party tag or an authorized smartphone. Communication with the module of the system occurs through the dialog code.

Hacking is excluded.
